Interactive Designs

I applied many interactive elements in this website. First, when your cursor hover on the banner item, it will change color and the text will

be highlighten. The first and second buttons of item area are hyperlinks to my CV and SoP documents. There is a slideshow area under

contact information. It will chage my projects every 5 second or you can use the next and previous buttons to change its.

Additionally, for users, they are not so familiar with Taiwanese universities and business sometimes. So I provided a hyperlink to FJU's

wikipedia page or the companies official-pages. Therefore, the users who are surfing on my portfolio page can intuitively click the school

emblems or business trademarks to gain more information.

Lastly, I added Javascript to make Top button back smoothly to top of this webpage. The user can click this button back to top without a sense

of abruptness. By adding this part of JS, it creates a more user-friendly experience.
